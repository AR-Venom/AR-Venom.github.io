Head Robot Driver

I am a sophomore at Alliance Academy for Innovation. I participated in robotics for three years at Vickery Creek Elementary from third grade to fifth grade. I did robotics in the sixth, seventh, and my freshman year of high school competitively. I went to state levels in seventh and ninth grade. I am on the team because I enjoy problem-solving and building things, which robotics requires a lot of. My favorite part about FIRST is the fact that everyone follows the rules, especially gracious professionalism making it an enjoyable and fun environment to be in.
