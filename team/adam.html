Adam Ewers

Mechanical Lead

I have done robotics for the last four years, and I am in tenth grade. The robotics programs I have done are Vex IQ, Vex EDR, FTC, and FRC. This is my second year in the FIRST program, and my favorite part of the program is being able to connect with other teams and get involved in the community with outreach. I joined robotics in sixth grade because I wanted to be an engineer, and robotics is a way to get experience in what engineering is like. I also enjoy being outside because I enjoy playing football, mountain biking, and ultimate frisbee. I am looking forward to a great FTC season!
